May 14th

Something Behind It

Recently, I have been watching the wallpaper in the evening. When the light changes, the pattern also shifts. I know it can not actually move. But something behind it does. A strange shape.

I have no explanation for such a movement. I told myself that I will stop looking at it but I can not.

John would be so furious if he knew how many hours I spent looking at it. I did not tell him. I did not tell anyone.
