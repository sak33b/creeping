The Pattern Doesn't Connect

June 15th

It has been a while since we came to this house. I tried to rest. But I could not. My mind keeps thinking about the wallpaper. The pattern seems inconsistent. It starts in one direction and breaks and begins somewhere else.

However, John says I am improving. He said that I should not let my imagination take over myself. I smiled and told him that indeed I am feeling much better. I did not mention him about the wallpaper. I am not sure why.
