May 18th

The Woman Within

I have not slept for days. I do not want to sleep. The shape behind the pattern seems clear now. It is a woman. She is pressed flat in the back of the paper. She is trying to push through.

During the day the pattern keeps her still. But at night, at night she shakes the pattern. It seems like she is trying to climb out of the pattern. I know how this sounds. Believe it or not I know. But I am absolutely sure that it is indeed a woman. I have watched it long enough to conclude. John says I am getting better. I did not correct him.
