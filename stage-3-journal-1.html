The Tearing Begins

July 4th... I think

Finally I took an initiative. I started to pull the wallpaper. Only at the edges where no one will notice.

I must help her get out. The smell of the wallpaper is also disgusting. Yellow and stale and creeping. Yet I can not stop myself from peeling. She needs to get out.

Now I understand why she shakes it every night. You simply can not wait behind something like that, can you? You have to break it and get out. I understand this completely. I understand this better than anything I have ever understood.

John came to me in the afternoon. I pretended to be asleep. I laid completely still and watched him having my eyes half closed. He looked at the wallpaper for a while. He said nothing and left the room.

He saw nothing. He has never seen anything.
