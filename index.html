The Yellow Room

June 14th

John has brought me to this house for summer. He said that fresh air is good for me and I should not be so worried. I should think less. I do not agree with his technique but he is a doctor. He knows what is best for me.

The room he picked for me is large. The windows are barred. There is wallpaper in the room. It is yellow and it is peeling at the edges. It has a strange pattern which I can not follow no matter how much I stare.
